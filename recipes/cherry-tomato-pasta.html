Cherry Tomato Pasta

This pasta dish is super easy and quick to make, but tastes amazing! It's perfect for a quick weeknight meal. You can substitute the fresh basil for dry, but I recommend trying to get fresh basil as it makes a big difference. You can also substitute the parmesan cheese with yeast nutrient to make this recipe vegan.

Ingredients

  • 2 pints cherry tomatoes
  • 3 cloves garlic, finely minced
  • 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
  • 10-12 leaves fresh basil, cut chiffonade
  • 1/2 box linguine, cooked al-dente
  • 1/4 cup parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp olive oil in a large skillet. Add the cherry tomatoes and cook on medium until softened.
  2. Add the garlic, salt, and crushed red pepper and cook until fragrant.
  3. Use the back of a spoon to carefully mash the tomatoes to release their juices.
  4. Continue cooking until the tomatoes continue to burst and create a sauce.
  5. Transfer the cooked pasta on top of the tomato sauce along with the basil and parmesan cheese. Reserve some pasta water and add here to thicken the sauce
  6. Toss to combine.
